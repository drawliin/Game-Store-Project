Ghost Of Tsushima

PS4 - XBOX - PC

Scenes

In the midst of ancient Japan, honor hangs by a thread. Rise, warrior, and be guided by the winds of destiny to wield the blade's power and become the Ghost of Tsushima. In a land ruled by tradition, the samurai's way has been shattered by the merciless invasion of the Mongol Empire. Jin Sakai, the last of his kind, must forsake the code to defend his people. The war for Tsushima has triggered a conflict that abandons the old ways, calling for a new path. Now, the guidance of the Ghost is bestowed upon those spurned by tradition, who seek justice beyond honor. Traverse the lush landscapes, confront the invaders, and stand against the looming shadows to reclaim the essence of Tsushima and become its silent guardian.
